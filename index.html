About Us

Built Right Construction
& Remodeling

Built Right Construction is a College Station based handyman and remodeling business built on one simple principle -- do the job right or don't do it at all. Owner and operator Connor Pustejovsky has spent years sharpening his skills across every corner of home construction and remodeling, from framing decks to finishing bathrooms, laying flooring to hanging cabinets.

Connor built his reputation the old fashioned way -- through word of mouth and repeat customers who trust him to show up, do quality work, and stand behind it. No shortcuts, no cutting corners, and no subbing out to someone less qualified. When you hire Built Right, you get Connor.

What We Do

Services

From ground up builds to interior finishing work, we handle it all with the same level of care and quality.

■

Decks & Fencing

Custom built decks and fences designed to last. Pressure treated lumber, solid framing, and clean finishes that hold up to Texas weather.

■

Flooring & Tile

Hardwood, laminate, vinyl plank, ceramic, and porcelain tile. Flawless installs with proper prep and clean grout lines every time.

■

Bathrooms

Full bathroom remodels from demo to finish. Tile, vanities, fixtures, and everything in between handled by one crew start to finish.

■

Painting & Drywall

Interior and exterior painting with clean lines and proper prep. Drywall repair, texture matching, and full new installs done right the first time.

■

Cabinets & Trim

Cabinet installation and trim work that fits clean and sits level. Crown molding, baseboards, casing, and custom cabinet setups.

■

Windows & Doors

Replacement windows and door installs done with proper sealing and finish. No drafts, no leaks, no callbacks.

■

Gutters

New gutter installation and repair. Proper pitch, clean seams, and downspout placement that actually moves water away from your foundation.

■

Exterior Work

Siding, fascia, soffit, and general exterior repairs. We keep your home protected from the outside in with quality materials and solid workmanship.
